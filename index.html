PAWS is people helping animals, plain and simple. They rehabilitate injured and orphaned wildlife while providing shelter and adoption opportunities for homeless cats and dogs. They also work on educating people to make a better world for animals and people. PAWS envisions a world where all people recognize the intrinsic value of animals and consistently make choices that demonstrate compassion and respect.
